About CJ Pet Solutions

CJ Pet Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a USA-based pet care provider offering non-life-threatening services such as grooming, bathing, pet sitting, walking, and tailored companionship for your furry friends.

Our mission is to make pet care simple, safe, and stress-free. With a dedicated team of animal lovers, we ensure every service is provided with compassion, professionalism, and reliability.

Whether you're away for the weekend or just need help keeping your pet well-groomed, CJ Pet Solutions is here to help.
